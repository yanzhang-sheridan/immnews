Subscribers to Google One Will Be Able to Auto Back Up Android Phones

    Daniel Johnson — September 16, 2019

    Those who have a Google One subscription will receive automatic backups for Android devices. The new capability builds on the existing cloud storage service and offers an important feature for customers to use. Google One will be able to save all the information on a person's Android phone, including texts, photos, videos and more. Additionally, the backup capabilities will be extended to all of the users on an account.

    The automatic phone backups could entice users to Choose Google's cloud storage over other services, as it offers a convenient way to protect mobile data.

    Subscriptions for Google One begin at a $3 per month price point, for 200GB of cloud storage. The storage service was initially introduced in 2018, and then different subscription options were added.
