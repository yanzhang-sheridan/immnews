OnePlus Challenges Developers with the 5G Apps of Tomorrow Program

OnePlus' '5G Apps of Tomorrow' program is designed to challenge developers in creating apps that are specifically reliable on 5G technology. 5G is an emerging technology that is slowly being implemented to multiple platforms and the inclusion of this through apps partners with EE and Qualcomm

OnePlus' CEO Pete Lau states his belief of how 5G will grow through three stages of development -- 5G 1.0, 5G 2.0, and 5G 3.0.' The 5G 1.0 looks to improve data transfer speed to transform cloud data into local data speeds. 5G 2.0 connects mature Ai to a wide range of devices, and 5G 3.0 will be boundless, interconnecting everything. The 5G Apps of Tomorrow program highlights 5G hardware more than software.
